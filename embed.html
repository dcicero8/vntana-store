Step 01 — Basic Embed

3D out of the box.
One link.

Everything you see — rotation, zoom, AR — comes from a single <iframe>. No SDK, no JavaScript, no build step. Works in Shopify, Webflow, WordPress, or any static page.

HTML 
<iframe
  src="https://embed.vntana.com/v2
    ?productUuid=ca4aebfc-ebc6-4e79-b4e7-8ce4bca4d58d
    &clientSlug=example-files
    &organizationSlug=DCicero"
  allow="xr-spatial-tracking"
  allowfullscreen
  width="100%"
  height="500"
></iframe>
  • Drag to rotate, pinch to zoom
  • Native AR on iOS & Android
  • QR code for desktop AR sharing
