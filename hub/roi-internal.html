Where the value comes from

Method & assumptions
  • Hourly rate = loaded cost ÷ productive hours/year.
  • Labor — Bottom-up: files × minutes ÷ 60 × rate. Top-down: efficiency% × loaded cost × people. Pick one to avoid double-counting.
  • Search — people × hrs/week × reduction% × weeks × rate.
  • Vendor & rework — eliminated spend + (incidents × hours × rate).
  • Net benefit = gross savings − VNTANA cost. ROI = net ÷ cost. Payback = cost ÷ (gross ÷ 12).
  • Defaults seeded from the MillerKnoll 3D DAM ROI review; override every field per prospect.
