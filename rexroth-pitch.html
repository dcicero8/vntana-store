What we'll build

An interactive 3D model of a generic, unbranded compact track loader / skid steer, with your Bosch Rexroth components built in as clickable hotspots. Click one and the actual component, built from your CAD, pops up to spin, zoom, and open into an exploded view or cross-section, with text, catalog images, and video explaining how it performs as part of the system.

This is one experience you own and reuse everywhere. VNTANA is a 3D Product Digital Asset Management platform: upload new engineering files and build out fresh interactive walkthroughs for your website, trade shows, and per-demo sales customization.

Your website

Self-serve, before a rep is ever involved

Embed the interactive skid-steer experience with a single line of code (iFrame or web component). Buyers explore and evaluate on their own: your mobile-hydraulics answer to the hydrogen showcase.

Sales & distributors

Walk the system live, no engineer on the call

Each rep and distribution partner gets a shareable link, and a "Showroom" grouping multiple products. Custom links per rep show you who's using it and how often.

Trade shows · IVT

Booth touchscreen, tablet, or the 75″ monitor

Runs on whatever's on the stand. If timing allows, we can stand up a lightweight version in time for IVT as a low-risk test.

Mobile marketing unit

Digital storytelling where machines can't fit

The roadshow truck can't carry full machines, which is exactly where this shines. In-depth exploded views and guided walkthroughs explain how your components run a machine and what sets them apart.

🛡️ How your IP stays protected

  • Internals stripped automatically: proprietary geometry and metadata removed before anything is shared externally. No engineer has to sanitize files.
  • SOC2 Type II certified: we pass enterprise security review as-is, collapsing procurement's check from months to paperwork.
