Why Build a Configurator

Configurable products create a visualization gap: the customer is buying something that doesn't exist yet. A 3D configurator closes that gap, and the numbers show what happens when it does.

2×

Conversion increase

Kohler e-commerce after deploying VNTANA 3D

Patagonia wholesale buyers on 3D product content

Months → Minutes

Sales cycle compression

YKK trim selection: multiple consultations to a single meeting

🛒 Purchase confidence → conversion

High-consideration products stall when buyers can't picture the final build. Seeing their exact combination in 3D, and in their own space via AR, removes the hesitation that kills carts.

📦 Fewer physical samples & returns

Every sample not produced and shipped is direct cost saved, and every accurate expectation set is a return avoided. YKK cut sample production and international shipping dramatically.

🧾 Error-free, self-serve ordering

The configurator carries the full order spec, every option, every code, so what the customer sees is exactly what operations builds. No transcription errors, no revision loops.

📈 Bigger baskets by design

A step-by-step builder naturally presents every add-on in context. "Add a side unit, wrap the corner, upgrade the finish" is upsell built into the buying experience, not bolted on.

🤝 From order-taker to design partner

Sales teams guide customers through possibilities in real time instead of quoting catalogs. YKK found this repositioned them from component supplier to development partner.

📊 Demand signal you can't get elsewhere

Every configuration session is market research: which combinations customers actually build, where they hesitate, what they abandon. Analytics integration comes standard.

The competitive landscape has moved

Configurators used to be a luxury-brand novelty. Today they're table stakes in every considered-purchase category, and customers arrive expecting the experience they got when they built their last car online.

Automotive & Powersports

Every major auto brand; Indian Motorcycle's build-your-bike flow is the reference experience.

Furniture & Home

Sofas, kitchens, closets, fixtures, configure-then-AR-place is now the default path.

Apparel & Components

Custom footwear builders; YKK brought it to B2B trim with VNTANA.

Outdoor Living

Modular grills and outdoor kitchens are exactly this kind of purchase, big ticket, highly configurable, spatial.

The brands that own the configuration experience own the customer relationship. The ones that don't are selling SKUs into someone else's experience.

YKK Trim Creator, the industry's first 3D trim configurator

For decades, apparel designers picked zippers from paper catalogs, ordered physical samples from distant factories, and waited weeks per revision. YKK, the world's largest fastener manufacturer, partnered with VNTANA to replace that cycle with real-time 3D.

  • ✓ Slider, pull, teeth, and tape configured with instant 3D feedback, millions of possible combinations
  • ✓ One-click changes to size, material, function, and color
  • ✓ Every build carries its exact item code, copy-paste into the ordering system, error-free
  • ✓ Consultation-to-confirmation in a single meeting; revisions in seconds instead of weeks
  • ✓ Physical sample production and international shipping costs cut dramatically

In Development · Retail

Michaels, custom framing configurator & AR

Custom framing is a high-value, personally meaningful purchase that customers historically couldn't visualize before committing. VNTANA is building Michaels a full 3D/AR configurator for the custom framing business across 1,200+ North American stores' online experience.

  • ✓ Real-time parametric sizing, frames rebuild to any custom dimension
  • ✓ Material swapping (paper, canvas, wood), 22 mat colors, single/double mats, glazing options
  • ✓ Customers upload their own artwork and see it framed before ordering
  • ✓ AR room viewer, place the configured frame on your actual wall from mobile
  • ✓ 300+ frame catalog, phased from the top-selling collection that drives 75% of revenue
