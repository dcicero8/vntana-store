Overview

The 007e ECM replaces the standard PSC motor with an electronically commutated motor (ECM) that automatically adjusts speed based on system demand. This means lower energy consumption during partial-load conditions — which is most of the time in a well-designed hydronic system.

Compatible with all existing 007 installations. Drop-in replacement with the same footprint, flange pattern, and connection sizes.

Applications

  • Residential hydronic heating
  • Radiant floor systems
  • Fan coil units
  • Domestic hot water recirculation
  • Solar thermal systems
  • Light commercial HVAC

Technology

ECM motors are brushless DC motors with a built-in inverter. Unlike PSC motors that run at fixed speed, ECM motors modulate continuously — reducing energy use by up to 85% compared to single-speed alternatives.

SureStart® monitors motor torque at startup and automatically reverses briefly to clear air locks. Bio Barrier® coating on wetted components inhibits biofilm growth in domestic recirculation applications.
